Research and Publications


I am a keen observer of the technology world and try to take part in its development through research. Over the summer of 2022, I worked on a project using deep learning models to develop a system which will enable a social robot ‘MISTY’ to communicate with deaf and dump people in American Sign Language. I worked on the project actively during the period of June 22- August 22 and have contributed to the initial investigation and research of the project. This project is still under development where members of my research team are trying to achieve the said goal.

I had an opportunity to explore the world of research in my undergrad years. I worked as a researcher for two years on power and energy systems. Prof (Dr) P.B. Karandikar took me as his assistant on his research project on supercapacitors. I presented our work in an international conference held in Bangalore, and later our work got published in IEEE Xplore. An international conference held at Delhi Technological University, India invited us to present our results gathered during our research work, again it got published in IEEE Xplore. This experience motivated me to appreciate the intricacies involved in solving problems with novel ideas and I believe this will help me in taking up extensive projects at my graduate study. Our work was recognised at various conferences and we were able to publish a few papers on the same.
